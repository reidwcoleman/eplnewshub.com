England vs. Netherlands

The semifinal between England and the Netherlands was a thrilling encounter that ended in a dramatic 2-1 victory for England. Held in Dortmund, the match saw the Netherlands take the lead with a goal from Xavi Simons in the 30th minute. England responded with a penalty by Harry Kane in the 45th minute, following a controversial VAR review for a foul on Kane by Denzel Dumfries. The decisive moment came in the dying minutes when Ollie Watkins scored the winning goal in stoppage time, securing England's place in the final​ (UEFA.com)​​ (Evening Standard)​​ (Sporting News)​.

Key Players and Performances

Xavi Simons: The young Dutch midfielder opened the scoring with a well-placed shot, showcasing his agility and precision. Despite the loss, Simons was a standout performer for the Netherlands, demonstrating why he is considered one of the brightest talents in European football.

Harry Kane: England's captain was pivotal in the semifinal, scoring the crucial equalizer from the penalty spot. Kane's leadership and experience were evident throughout the match, guiding the team through tense moments.

Ollie Watkins: The hero of the match, Watkins' last-minute goal was a testament to his composure and clinical finishing. His performance has solidified his place as a key player for England in this tournament.

Interviews and Reactions

After the match, Harry Kane spoke to the press about the team's resilience and determination. "It was a tough game, but we showed great character to come back. The support from our fans has been incredible, and we’re thrilled to reach the final," Kane said​ (Evening Standard)​.

Ollie Watkins, the match-winner, expressed his joy and disbelief at scoring the decisive goal. "It’s a dream come true. I’ve always wanted to play in big matches like this, and to score the winning goal is just unbelievable," Watkins remarked​ (UEFA.com)​.

Spain vs. France

In the other semifinal, Spain triumphed over France with a 2-1 victory in Munich. Lamine Yamal opened the scoring for Spain in the 25th minute with a brilliant solo effort, dribbling past several defenders before slotting the ball into the net. France equalized through Kolo Muani in the 60th minute, but Spain regained the lead with a late goal from Dani Olmo in the 80th minute, ensuring their progression to the final​ (UEFA.com)​​ (UEFA.com)​​ (UEFA.com)​.

Key Players and Performances

Lamine Yamal: The 17-year-old sensation has been a revelation in this tournament. His goal against France was a display of skill and confidence, further establishing him as one of the tournament’s breakout stars.

Kolo Muani: France's equalizer came from Muani, who has been a consistent performer throughout the tournament. His physical presence and ability to score crucial goals have made him a key player for Les Bleus.

Dani Olmo: Scoring the winning goal, Olmo's performance was crucial for Spain. His creativity and vision were on full display, making him one of the standout players of the match.

Interviews and Reactions

Lamine Yamal, after his stunning performance, spoke about his experience in the tournament. "Playing in Euro 2024 has been a fantastic experience. Scoring in the semifinals is something I will never forget. We have a great team, and we’re ready for the final," Yamal said​ (UEFA.com)​.

Dani Olmo, who scored the decisive goal, highlighted the team’s spirit and determination. "This team never gives up. We knew we had to be at our best against France, and everyone gave their all. Now we’re focused on winning the final," Olmo stated​ (UEFA.com)​.

Final Matchup

The final will see England facing Spain in Berlin on July 14th. Both teams have shown exceptional skill and determination throughout the tournament, setting the stage for an exciting conclusion to Euro 2024​ (UEFA.com)​​ (UEFA.com)​.

Team Preparations and Expectations

England’s coach Gareth Southgate has emphasized the importance of focus and preparation ahead of the final. "We’ve come a long way, but the job isn’t finished yet. We need to stay focused and prepare well for the final. Spain is a strong team, and we respect them, but we’re confident in our abilities," Southgate commented​ (Sporting News)​.

Spain’s coach Luis de la Fuente spoke about the team’s journey and their readiness for the final. "This has been an incredible tournament for us. The players have shown great commitment and quality. We’re ready for the final, and we’ll give everything to win," de la Fuente said​ (UEFA.com)​.

Conclusion

The semifinals of Euro 2024 provided fans with intense competition and memorable moments. England's late heroics and Spain's tactical brilliance have set up a highly anticipated final that fans around the world are eager to witness.
