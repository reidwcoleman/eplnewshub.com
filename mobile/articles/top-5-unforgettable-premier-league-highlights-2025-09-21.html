The 8-2 thrashing handed out by Manchester United to Arsenal in August 2011 is a match that remains etched in the annals of Premier League highlights for its sheer shock value and the statement it made. From the outset, Manchester United were ruthless, with Wayne Rooney orchestrating the attack and scoring a hat-trick that left the Arsenal defense in tatters.
