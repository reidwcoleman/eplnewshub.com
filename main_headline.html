Marcus Rashford's Barcelona Move Shakes Premier League Transfer Window

Marcus Rashford's stunning season-long loan move to Barcelona from Manchester United headlines the most explosive transfer window in Premier League history. The 27-year-old England international joins the Catalan giants with a €30m option to buy, while clubs across the league spend over £1 billion on new signings. Liverpool's record £100m capture of Florian Wirtz, Arsenal's pursuit of Viktor Gyökeres, and Manchester United's £70m Bryan Mbeumo signing reshape the title race as the 2025/26 season approaches.
