Can Arsenal Dethrone Manchester City As Premier League Champions

Arsenal begins the 2024/25 Premier League season with renewed hope, believing this could finally be the year they dethrone Manchester City, who have dominated English football for the last few seasons. Arsenal’s resurgence under manager Mikel Arteta has been steady, and after two consecutive seasons of finishing as runners-up to City, the Gunners are determined to go one better this time. But with Pep Guardiola’s side still as formidable as ever, Arsenal’s path to the title is anything but straightforward.
