Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upgrade to premium membership?
Upgrading is simple – just click the "Upgrade to Premium" button in your account dashboard or visit the membership page. Choose your preferred billing cycle, enter your payment information, and you'll have instant access to all premium features. The upgrade process takes less than two minutes.

Can I share my account with family members?
Each EPL News Hub account is intended for individual use to ensure personalized recommendations work correctly. However, we offer family plans at a discounted rate that allow multiple family members to have their own accounts while sharing a single billing arrangement.

What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?
If you cancel your premium subscription, you'll revert to a free account but retain access to your reading history, bookmarks, and preferences. You can reactivate your premium membership at any time and pick up right where you left off. We maintain your data for 12 months after cancellation before permanent deletion.

How often is premium content updated?
Premium content is updated continuously throughout the day. We publish at least 5-10 exclusive premium articles daily, with increased coverage during transfer windows and crucial periods of the season. Premium members also get early access to all standard content, typically seeing articles 2-4 hours before they're publicly available.

Can I change my favorite teams after signing up?
Absolutely! Your preferences can be updated at any time through your account settings. You can follow multiple teams, adjust your content priorities, and modify notification settings whenever your interests change. The system will immediately adapt to show relevant content based on your new preferences.
