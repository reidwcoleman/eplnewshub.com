Euro 2024 Round of 16: Which Teams Are Moving On?

The Round of 16 at Euro 2024 featured several exciting matches with top teams battling for a place in the quarter-finals. Here are the key highlights and results from these games:

Germany vs. Denmark

Germany secured a 2-0 victory over Denmark, with goals from Kai Havertz (53' pen) and Jamal Musiala (68').

England vs. Slovakia

England triumphed 2-1 over Slovakia in a match that went into extra time. Jude Bellingham scored in the 90'+5 minute and Harry Kane added another in the 91st minute. Slovakia's Ivan Schranz scored in the 25th minute.

Switzerland vs. Italy

Switzerland pulled off a surprise 2-0 win against Italy. Goals from Remo Freuler (37') and Ruben Vargas (46') helped Switzerland advance.

France vs. Belgium

France narrowly defeated Belgium 1-0, with an own goal from Jan Vertonghen (85').

Other Notable Matches

  • Spain vs. Georgia: Spain dominated Georgia with a 4-1 win, featuring goals from Rodri (39'), Fabián Ruiz (51'), Nico Williams (75'), and Dani Olmo (83').
  • Portugal vs. Slovenia: The match ended 0-0 but Portugal won 3-0 in the penalty shootout, thanks to a stellar performance by goalkeeper Diogo Costa.
  • Romania vs. Netherlands: The Netherlands advanced with a convincing 3-0 victory over Romania, with goals from Cody Gakpo (20') and Donyell Malen (83', 90+3').
  • Austria vs. Türkiye: Türkiye edged Austria 2-1, with goals from Merih Demiral (1', 59') and Michael Gregoritsch (66').

Conclusion

These results have set up an intriguing quarter-final stage, with several high-profile clashes on the horizon.
