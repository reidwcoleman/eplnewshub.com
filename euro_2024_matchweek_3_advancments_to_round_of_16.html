Euro 2024 Matchweek 3: Advancements to the Round of 16

As Matchweek 3 of Euro 2024 concluded, the group stage came to an exhilarating end, setting the stage for the Round of 16. Here’s a comprehensive look at the results, the teams that advanced from each group, and the fixtures for the knockout stage.

Group A

Germany emerged as the dominant force in Group A, securing their spot in the Round of 16 with a perfect record. They topped the group after defeating Hungary 2-0 and drawing 1-1 with Switzerland. Switzerland also progressed by securing the second spot after their draws with Germany and Scotland. Hungary claimed the third spot, advancing as one of the best third-placed teams after a crucial 1-0 victory over Scotland in the final match.

Group B

Spain dominated Group B, winning all their matches, including a 3-0 win over Croatia and a tight 1-0 victory against Italy. Italy secured second place despite a challenging campaign, while Croatia, with two draws and a loss, was unable to progress, marking a surprising early exit for the 2018 World Cup finalists.

Group C

England topped Group C, securing their place in the knockout stage with a win and two draws. Denmark followed closely, advancing in second place with three draws. Slovenia, despite also having three draws, moved forward as one of the best third-placed teams, highlighting the competitive nature of this group.

Group D

Austria emerged victorious in Group D, finishing first with two wins and a loss. France, showcasing consistent performance, took the second spot with one win and two draws. The Netherlands advanced as one of the best third-placed teams, thanks to their win against Poland and a draw against France.

Group E

Group E saw a tightly contested battle with Romania, Belgium, and Slovakia all finishing with identical records. Romania emerged top on goal difference, followed by Belgium in second place. Slovakia's consistent performance earned them a place in the knockout stages as one of the best third-placed teams.

Group F

Portugal topped Group F, showcasing their strength with victories over Turkey and Czechia. Turkey followed in second place, while Czechia, despite a challenging campaign, secured their advancement as one of the best third-placed teams.

Round of 16 Fixtures

The Round of 16 matches are set to begin on June 29, featuring exciting clashes across various cities in Germany. Here’s a detailed look at the upcoming fixtures and the teams involved:

  • Germany (1A) vs Denmark (2C) - 18:00 CEST, Berlin (Olympiastadion)
  • Spain (1B) vs Hungary (3A) - 21:00 CEST, Munich (Allianz Arena)

June 30, 2024:

  • England (1C) vs Netherlands (3D) - 12:00 CEST, Stuttgart (MHPArena)

July 1, 2024:
