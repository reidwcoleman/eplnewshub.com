Premier League 2024/25 Schedule: Key Fixtures and Upcoming Games

The 2024/25 Premier League season is set to kick off on August 16, 2024, with Manchester United hosting Fulham. This season promises intense competition, featuring 380 games played across 33 weekends, four midweek rounds, and one Bank Holiday Matchweek. The season will conclude on May 25, 2025, with all final-day matches kicking off simultaneously to ensure fairness in the race for the title and European spots.

Opening Weekend Highlights

Friday, August 16, 2024: The season opener sees Manchester United facing Fulham at Old Trafford.
Saturday, August 17, 2024: Newly promoted Ipswich Town will test their mettle against Liverpool. Arsenal takes on Wolves, while Everton faces Brighton.
Sunday, August 18, 2024: Defending champions Manchester City visit Chelsea, a match that is expected to be an early test of both teams' title credentials.
Monday, August 19, 2024: Leicester City plays Tottenham Hotspur in what promises to be an exciting clash.

Notable Fixtures in Early Weeks

Matchweek 2 (August 24-25, 2024): Manchester City hosts Ipswich Town, and Brighton welcomes Manchester United. Aston Villa takes on Arsenal, promising an exciting encounter.
Matchweek 3 (August 31-September 1, 2024): West Ham faces Manchester City, while Arsenal plays Brighton. Manchester United and Liverpool's clash will undoubtedly be one of the highlights of the early season.
Matchweek 4 (September 14-15, 2024): The North London derby sees Tottenham Hotspur hosting Arsenal. Liverpool will play Nottingham Forest, and Manchester City meets Brentford.
Matchweek 5 (September 21-22, 2024): West Ham hosts Chelsea, and Aston Villa takes on Wolves. Liverpool will play AFC Bournemouth at Anfield.

Mid-Season Highlights

Matchweek 10 (November 2, 2024): Newcastle United vs. Arsenal - An important match for both teams' aspirations.
Matchweek 12 (November 23, 2024): Manchester City vs. Tottenham Hotspur - Another critical match in the title race.
Matchweek 16 (December 14, 2024): Manchester City hosts Manchester United in the Manchester derby.
Matchweek 18 (December 26, 2024): Arsenal faces Ipswich Town, while Chelsea takes on Fulham in a London derby.

End of Season

The final day of the season, known as Championship Sunday, will be on May 25, 2025, with all matches kicking off simultaneously. This ensures that no team has an advantage in the final push for the title, European places, or to avoid relegation.

With newly promoted teams Leicester City, Ipswich Town, and Southampton eager to prove their worth in the top flight, and established powerhouses like Manchester City, Liverpool, and Chelsea vying for supremacy, the 2024/25 Premier League season promises to be one of the most thrilling yet.
