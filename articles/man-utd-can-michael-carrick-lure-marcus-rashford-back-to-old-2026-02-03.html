Man Utd: Can Michael Carrick Lure Marcus Rashford Back to Old Trafford?

With Michael Carrick in the running for the permanent manager position, a Rashford reunion could be on the cards. But can he persuade the England star to return to his boyhood club?

By Emily Wilson
3 February 2026 · 12 min read

As the managerial merry-go-round continues to spin at Manchester United, one name has emerged as a serious contender for the top job: Michael Carrick. The former United midfielder has been touted as a potential successor to Erik ten Hag, and his potential appointment has significant implications for the club's transfer plans. Specifically, it could pave the way for a sensational return to Old Trafford for England star Marcus Rashford.

The Carrick Factor

According to reports from Sky Sports, Goal.com, and the Manchester Evening News, Michael Carrick is keen to bring Marcus Rashford back to Manchester United if he lands the permanent job. This is a tantalizing prospect for United fans, who have watched Rashford blossom into one of the Premier League's most feared forwards since his departure from the club. But what makes Carrick think he can persuade Rashford to return to his boyhood club?

One possible factor is the pair's existing relationship. Carrick has worked with Rashford during his time as a coach at United, and the two are believed to have a strong rapport. This could give Carrick an edge in negotiations, particularly if Rashford is weighing up his options and considering a move back to the Premier League.

Barcelona's Trump Card

However, Manchester United are not the only club in the running for Rashford's signature. Barcelona are also reportedly interested in signing the England star, and according to Goal.com, they hold a trump card in the transfer battle. While the exact nature of this trump card is unclear, it's likely that Barcelona's reputation as one of the world's biggest clubs, combined with their impressive squad and attractive style of play, make them a compelling option for Rashford.

This puts the pressure on Michael Carrick and Manchester United to make a convincing case for Rashford's return. They will need to persuade him that Old Trafford is the best place for him to develop his career, both on and off the pitch. This could involve offering Rashford a key role in the team, as well as a competitive salary and a clear vision for the club's future.

Rashford's Career Crossroads

Marcus Rashford is currently at a crossroads in his career. He has established himself as one of the Premier League's top forwards, but he may be looking for a new challenge. A move to Barcelona would offer him the opportunity to test himself in a new league and compete for major honors, including the Champions League. On the other hand, a return to Manchester United would allow him to reconnect with his roots and play for a club that is close to his heart.

Rashford's decision will not be taken lightly, and he will likely weigh up his options carefully. He will consider factors such as the style of play, the squad, and the manager, as well as the club's ambitions and expectations. Ultimately, the decision will come down to what he believes is best for his career and his personal development.

Carrick's Masterplan

While the exact details of Michael Carrick's masterplan for Manchester United are unclear, it's likely that he has a clear vision for the club's future. This could involve building a team around Rashford, as well as other key players, and implementing a style of play that is both attractive and effective.

Carrick's experience as a player and coach at United will serve him well in this regard. He knows the club inside out and has a deep understanding of what it takes to succeed at the highest level. He will be able to use this knowledge to inform his decision-making and build a team that is capable of competing with the best in the Premier League.

What Next for Manchester United?

As the situation continues to unfold, Manchester United fans will be watching with bated breath. The prospect of Marcus Rashford returning to the club is a tantalizing one, and it could be the catalyst for a new era of success at Old Trafford.

However, the club must be cautious not to get ahead of themselves. The transfer market is unpredictable, and there are no guarantees that Rashford will choose to return to United. The club must be prepared for all eventualities and have a clear plan in place, regardless of the outcome.

A Rashford reunion could be on the cards, but can Michael Carrick persuade the England star to return to his boyhood club?

In the meantime, Manchester United will continue to monitor the situation and wait for developments. The club's fans will be hoping that Carrick's appointment will be the catalyst for a new era of success, and that Rashford's return will be the icing on the cake.

Conclusion and Future Prospects

As the Premier League season continues to unfold, Manchester United's future prospects are shrouded in uncertainty. However, one thing is clear: the club is at a crossroads, and the decisions made in the coming weeks and months will have a significant impact on their future success.

With Michael Carrick in the running for the permanent manager position, and Marcus Rashford potentially returning to the club, the future is looking bright for Manchester United. But only time will tell if the club can capitalize on this momentum and achieve their goals.

The prospect of Marcus Rashford returning to Manchester United is a tantalizing one, and it could be the catalyst for a new era of success at Old Trafford.

In the end, it all comes down to the decisions made by the club's hierarchy, as well as the players themselves. If Manchester United can persuade Rashford to return, and Carrick can implement his vision for the club, then the future could be very bright indeed.
