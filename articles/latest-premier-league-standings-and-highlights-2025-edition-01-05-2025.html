Latest Premier League Standings: A Thrilling Race at Both Ends

Top of the Table

1. Liverpool
Leading the Premier League with 45 points from 18 matches, Liverpool continues to showcase a strong campaign. Their recent 2-2 draw against Manchester United at Anfield was a gripping encounter where Mohamed Salah equaled Thierry Henry's goal record. Despite the draw, Liverpool maintains a six-point lead at the top.

2. Arsenal
Sitting in second with 40 points from 20 matches, Arsenal narrowly missed a chance to close the gap on Liverpool. A 1-1 draw against Brighton & Hove Albion highlighted their struggles to capitalize on key moments, but the Gunners remain firmly in the title race.

3. Nottingham Forest
One of the season's biggest surprises, Nottingham Forest is in third place with 37 points from 19 matches. Their performances have been a revelation, positioning them as unexpected contenders for European football.
