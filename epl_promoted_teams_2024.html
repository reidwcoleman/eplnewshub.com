Newly Promoted Teams in the Premier League 2024/25 Season

The 2024/25 Premier League season welcomes three newly promoted teams: Leicester City, Ipswich Town, and Southampton. Each club has a unique story of resilience and ambition as they return to England's top flight.

Leicester City

Leicester City secured their promotion as champions of the EFL Championship. Their journey back to the Premier League was confirmed after a dominant season where they accumulated 97 points, with standout performances throughout the campaign. The Foxes, who were relegated just a year ago, made a strategic move by appointing Enzo Maresca, previously an assistant coach at Manchester City, as their manager. His tactical acumen proved pivotal in guiding Leicester back to the top tier. Ipswich Town

Ipswich Town achieved automatic promotion by finishing second in the Championship with 96 points. This promotion is particularly significant as it marks their return to the Premier League after a 22-year absence. Under the management of Kieran McKenna, Ipswich demonstrated consistency and strength, culminating in crucial victories towards the end of the season. Notably, Ipswich became only the fifth team in history to achieve back-to-back promotions from the third tier to the Premier League, showcasing their rapid rise through the divisions.

Southampton

Southampton clinched their spot in the Premier League through the playoffs, defeating Leeds United 1-0 in the final. The Saints showed resilience throughout the season and in the high-pressure environment of the playoffs. Under the guidance of Russell Martin, Southampton's tactical approach and solid defense were instrumental in their successful campaign. This promotion sees Southampton bounce back after a year in the Championship, aiming to reestablish themselves in the Premier League.
