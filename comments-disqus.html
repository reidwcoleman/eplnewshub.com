Option 1: Disqus (Recommended - Free & Instant)

Step 1: Create a Disqus Account

1. Go to disqus.com/profile/signup

2. Sign up with your email or Google account

3. Choose "I want to install Disqus on my site"

Step 2: Register Your Site

1. Website Name: EPL News Hub

2. Unique Disqus URL (shortname): eplnewshub

3. Category: Sports

4. Choose the Basic (Free) plan

Step 3: Get Your Embed Code

1. Select "Universal Code" as your platform

2. Copy this code and save it as comments-disqus.js:
